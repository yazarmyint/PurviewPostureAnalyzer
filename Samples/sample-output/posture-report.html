Observations

Advisory only — for the consultant to weigh against engagement context, licensing, and stakeholder input. Not decisions, not a remediation plan.

DLP looks broader than it enforces

6 policies read as substantial coverage, but 2 never left test mode and Teams is entirely unscoped.

→ Worth confirming which locations the client believes are protected vs. what's actually enforcing.

Insider-risk exposure exists, but it's a scoping conversation first

No IRM coverage for departing-employee data theft. IRM is an E5-tier workload and needs HR/Legal alignment before any policy is scoped.

→ Raise as a scoping + licensing item, not a quick config win.

Copilot is live ahead of its guardrails

Copilot is deployed while DSPM-for-AI is audit-only and labels aren't enforced on AI-reachable content.

→ Validate oversharing exposure early in the engagement.
